### Task 1: hallucination accounting

Across **2,304 LLM buy decisions** under v1 (free-form prompt with a buggy
validator) and **2,288** under v2 (structured `STATE/ECHO/REASON/ANSWER`
prompt with deterministic per-field validator).

| | v1 | v2 |
|---|---:|---:|
| LLM calls (total) | 2,304 | 2,288 |
| Calls flagged by validator | 100 (4.3%) | **0 (0.0%)** |
| Real first-pass hallucinations | **0** | **0** |
| Retry-induced fabrications (subset of flagged) | 47 | 0 |
| Spurious-only validator artefacts (subset of flagged) | 53 | 0 |

The 47 "retry-induced fabrications" and 53 "spurious-only" together
partition the 100 flagged calls in v1; they do not double-count.

**Source files:**
- `notes/task1_postmortem_2026-04-29.md` (full breakdown + reclassification)
- `logs/llm_eval/2p_v2/decisions/*.jsonl`, `logs/llm_eval/3p_v2/decisions/*.jsonl`

**Figure:** `report/figures/llm/fig_v1_vs_v2_hallucination.png`.

### Task 2: LLM-GA winner cross-evaluation under the rule-based pool

The LLM-driven GA-2p winner re-evaluated under the static 30-strategy
rule-based pool (1,000 games × 10 matchups, both 2p and 3p).

| metric | 2p eval | 3p eval |
|---|---:|---:|
| score | 1.045 | 1.015 |
| mean fairness $\overline{F}$ | **0.379** | **0.404** |
| max-pair fairness $F_{\max}$ | 0.990 | 0.640 |
| mean rounds | 45.9 | 47.1 |
| mean draw rate | 0.003 | 0.008 |
| mean transfer ($/round) | 82.4 | 160.6 |

Under the LLM evaluator (the LLM-GA winner's "native" eval at 2 identical seats):

| metric | LLM eval |
|---|---:|
| 2p fairness $\overline{F}$ | 0.20 |
| 3p fairness $\overline{F}$ | 0.05 |
| 2p rounds | 44 |
| 3p rounds | 36 |

The cross-evaluator fairness gap on the LLM-GA winner is **+0.18 at 2p**
($0.379 − 0.200$) and **+0.35 at 3p** ($0.404 − 0.050$); the gap widens
with more identical seats, which is the headline diagnostic of the paper.

**Source files:**
- `logs/optimizer/cross_eval_llm_ga_winner.json` (rule-based pool eval)
- `logs/llm_eval/llm_ga_winner_3p/summary.csv` (LLM eval at 3p, n=20)
- LLM 2p eval values come from the LLM-GA training run itself
  (`logs/optimizer_llm/llm_ga_2p/evals.jsonl`)

**Figures:** `report/figures/llm/fig_cross_evaluator_gap.png`,
`report/figures/llm/fig_fairness_asymmetry.png`.
